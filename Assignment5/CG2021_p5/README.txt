How to use: 
    Shader selection:
        press 1: use the phong shader wth diffuse modulation
        press 2: use the phong shader with specular modulation (and the above)
        press 3: use the phong shader with normal texture (and the above)
        press 4: use the phong shader with material properties (and the above)

    Camera controls:
        Arrow keys: move camera in x and y axis
        F, G: control field of view
        8, 9, 0: change mesh to look at

    Light controls:
        W, S: move the current selected light in the y axis 
        A, D: move the current selected light in the x axis
    
    Mesh controls:
        spacebar: rotate the meshes
